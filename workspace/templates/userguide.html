Welcome to DogGo's User-Friendly User Guide

To start please navigate to our homepage
It should look a little something like this
Now go ahead and click the "Get Started!" under the "A Playground For Your Dog"
It should bring you to a registration page that looks like this
Now go ahead and fill in the boxes which ask for different things such as your username, password, etc
Please understand that there are some requirements such as having an age of atleast 16 and a valid email
The website should tell you if an error relating to your registration has occurred
An example of this might look like:
In this example above, your password was less than 8 characters in length
Don't worry this can be easily fixed, just type in a new password that is atleast 8 characters in length.
After you have successfully registered it should automatically bring you to a page where you can view your profile
Here you have access to many thing such as your about me, your ratings, your gallery, your information, and your preset profile picture
Lets begin by changing our profile picture and adding pictures to our gallery
Click your preset profile picture to pull up a menu that allows you to change your profile picture
It should look like this
Now you just click on the "Choose File" button under the "Profile pic" and the "gallery 1,2,3,4"
After selecting all of the pictures that you want on your profile, click any button on the right side such as the "profilePic" button
This will then upload your picturesand if you reload your page, you will most likely see the updated pictures
Now lets go on and move to the "Listings Page"
Look on the top right of your screen and you will see your profile picture in the shape of a ball
After you click that, go on and click the "your listings"
It should take you to our listings page which looks like this
Here you can see the listings that other users have placed and you also have permission to post your own post
To create your own post go ahead and click the "Create a Post" button.
You should show up to a page that looks like this
Now all you need to do is fill in the blanks and fulfill the requirements that come with it just like you did on the register page
After that you should be brought back to the listings page and voila, your post is on the tippity top of the page
And as always, to secure your account, please click the icon on the top right again and press "Sign out" after you are done using our services
Thats all for the User-Friendly User Guide
